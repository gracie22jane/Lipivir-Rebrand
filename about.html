About The Product

Lipivir® is the new lip gel product that gives your lips the care they deserve - and can reduce the frequency of cold sores. Because looking after your lips is the best way to prevent unsightly and painful blisters. Now you can count on lipivir® with its innovative formula to help put your lip worries behind you. lipivir® is proudly cruelty free and vegan.

How It Works

When the HSV1 herpes virus is dormant it sits in the ganglion nerve tissue. It sends out scout virus particles to investigate the condition of the lip cells. If the conditions are favourable, the scout virus particles send back signals to the herpes virus in the nerve tissue. Lipivir® interrupts this viral signalling, meaning that the virus particles around the lip cells, or the affected area that the product is applied to, cannot inform the herpes virus in the ganglion nerve tissue. This helps prevent an invasion of new virus particles, and therefore a cold sore outbreak is impeded.
Put simply, lipivir® builds a barrier – an internal barrier against the viral signalling that causes cold sores.
